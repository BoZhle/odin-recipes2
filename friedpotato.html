Oven-Fried Potato Chips

Ingredients

  • 4 russet potatoes, peeled and sliced diagonally
  • 4 tablespoons butter, melted
  • Kosher salt, to taste

Method

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the baking sheet
  2. Soak the potato slices, then pat dry
  3. Put the slices on a baking sheet, then brush with butter
  4. Lower the temperature and bake
  5. Sprinkle with salt and serve
