Oat Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup pepitas
  • 1/4 cup walnuts
  • 1/4 cup almonds
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3/4 cup raisins, dried apricots

Method

  1. Heat the oven
  2. Toast the seeds and nuts
  3. Grind 1/2 cup of the oats
  4. Mix the dough
  5. Stir in oats, nuts and dried fruits
  6. Drop the cookies onto the baking sheed
  7. Bake the cookies
